FAQS

Can you put sliding doors on built-in wardrobes?

Yes, you can definitely put sliding doors on built-in wardrobes. Sliding doors can be a great option for maximizing space and adding a modern touch to your wardrobe.

What are the benefits of using sliding doors for built-in wardrobes?

Sliding doors offer several benefits for built-in wardrobes. They save space since they don't swing open like hinged doors, making them ideal for smaller rooms. They also provide a sleek and modern look, enhance accessibility, and allow for easier organization of your wardrobe contents.

Are sliding doors suitable for all types of built-in wardrobes?

Sliding doors can be used for most types of built-in wardrobes, including those with standard dimensions and custom designs. However, it's important to ensure that your wardrobe structure is compatible with sliding doors and that there is enough space for the doors to slide open and closed smoothly.

Can I install sliding doors on an existing built-in wardrobe?

Yes, it is possible to install sliding doors on an existing built-in wardrobe. However, it's recommended to consult with a professional to assess the feasibility and make any necessary adjustments to ensure a proper fit and functionality.

What types of sliding doors are available for built-in wardrobes?

There are various types of sliding doors available for built-in wardrobes, including mirrored doors, glass doors, wood-paneled doors, and even customized options. The choice depends on your personal preferences and the style you want to achieve.

Can I customize the design of sliding doors for my built-in wardrobe?

Yes, sliding doors for built-in wardrobes can be customized to suit your style and preferences. You can choose different materials, finishes, colors, and patterns to match your interior décor and create a unique look.

How do I maintain sliding doors on built-in wardrobes?

Sliding doors are generally low-maintenance. Regularly wipe them down with a mild cleaner and soft cloth to remove any dust or fingerprints. Lubricate the tracks occasionally to ensure smooth sliding motion. Avoid slamming the doors or placing heavy objects on them to prevent damage.

Can I convert my existing hinged doors into sliding doors for my built-in wardrobe?

In some cases, it may be possible to convert hinged doors into sliding doors for built-in wardrobes. However, this would require modifications to the wardrobe structure and may not be feasible for all situations. It's advisable to consult with a professional to determine if this conversion is possible in your specific case.
