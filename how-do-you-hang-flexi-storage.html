FAQS

What tools do I need to hang flexi storage?

To hang flexi storage, you will typically need a drill, screws, a level, a measuring tape, and a pencil to mark the wall.

What is the best height to hang flexi storage?

The height to hang flexi storage depends on your personal preference and the items you want to store. Generally, it is recommended to hang it at a height that is convenient for you to reach and suits the items you plan to store.

How do I find the studs in the wall for hanging flexi storage?

To find the studs, you can use a stud finder, which is a handheld device that detects the wooden beams behind your walls. Alternatively, you can tap on the wall lightly with your knuckles, and if you hear a hollow sound, you are in between studs. If you hear a solid sound, you have found a stud.

Can I hang flexi storage without drilling holes in the wall?

Yes, there are alternative methods to hang flexi storage without drilling holes. You can use adhesive hooks or hanging strips designed for this purpose. However, keep in mind that the weight capacity of these alternatives may be limited compared to drilling into studs.

How do I ensure the flexi storage is level when hanging it?

To ensure the flexi storage is level, use a level tool. Place it on top of the storage unit and adjust it until the bubble in the level is centered. This will help you achieve a straight and balanced installation.

Can flexi storage be hung on any type of wall?

Flexi storage can typically be hung on various types of walls, including drywall, plaster, or concrete. However, the method of installation may vary depending on the wall surface. It is essential to use the appropriate wall anchors or screws suitable for the specific wall type.

Are there any weight limitations for flexi storage?

The weight capacity of flexi storage depends on the specific product and the method of installation. It is essential to follow the manufacturer's guidelines for weight limits to ensure the storage unit is securely hung and can support the intended items.

How can I make sure the flexi storage is secure and won't fall off the wall?

To ensure the flexi storage is secure, make sure to use the appropriate hardware, such as screws or wall anchors, based on the weight and size of the storage unit. It is important to install them properly into studs or use the recommended installation method for your specific wall type.

Can I hang flexi storage on a door?

Yes, it is possible to hang flexi storage on a door. There are various over-the-door organizers specifically designed for this purpose. These organizers typically hook over the top of the door and provide storage compartments or hooks.

How can I maximize space when hanging flexi storage?

To maximize space when hanging flexi storage, consider using vertical space by installing multiple units one above the other. Additionally, utilize the inside of doors, closet walls, or any available wall space in your home to hang flexi storage and create more storage options.
