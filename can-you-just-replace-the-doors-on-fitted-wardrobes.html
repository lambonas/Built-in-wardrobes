FAQS

Can you just replace the doors on fitted wardrobes?

Yes, you can easily replace the doors on fitted wardrobes without having to replace the entire unit.

Why would I want to replace the doors on my fitted wardrobes?

There are several reasons to replace the doors on your fitted wardrobes, including updating the style, refreshing the look of your bedroom, or to match a new interior design theme.

Can I replace the doors on my fitted wardrobe myself?

Yes, replacing the doors on a fitted wardrobe can be a DIY project if you have basic carpentry skills. However, it may be best to hire a professional if you are unsure or if you want a more complex design.

Do I need to measure my fitted wardrobe before replacing the doors?

Yes, it is important to accurately measure the dimensions of your existing fitted wardrobe before purchasing new doors to ensure a proper fit.

What materials are available for fitted wardrobe doors?

Fitted wardrobe doors come in a variety of materials, including solid wood, MDF, glass, mirrored panels, and even vinyl-wrapped finishes.

Can I change the style of my fitted wardrobe doors?

Yes, changing the style of your fitted wardrobe doors can completely transform the look of your bedroom. You can choose from various styles, such as traditional, modern, contemporary, or even customized designs.

Can I replace the doors on my fitted wardrobe without removing the entire unit?

In most cases, you can replace the doors on your fitted wardrobe without removing the entire unit. However, the process may vary depending on the specific design and structure of your wardrobe.

How much does it cost to replace the doors on a fitted wardrobe?

The cost of replacing the doors on a fitted wardrobe can vary depending on factors such as the size, material, design, and whether you hire a professional or do it yourself. It is best to obtain quotes from different suppliers or contractors to get an accurate estimate.

Are there any maintenance requirements for fitted wardrobe doors?

The maintenance requirements for fitted wardrobe doors will depend on the materials used. However, in general, regular cleaning and occasional lubrication of hinges or tracks may be necessary to keep the doors functioning smoothly.

Can I customize the doors on my fitted wardrobe?

Yes, many suppliers offer customization options for fitted wardrobe doors, allowing you to choose the color, finish, style, and even add additional features such as handles or decorative elements.
