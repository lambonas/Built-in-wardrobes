FAQS

What is a Kaboodle kitchen?

A Kaboodle kitchen is a DIY kitchen solution offered by Bunnings Warehouse. It consists of pre-cut cabinets and accessories that can be assembled and installed by homeowners.

How much does a Kaboodle kitchen cost?

The cost of a Kaboodle kitchen will vary depending on the size, style, and accessories chosen. It is recommended to use the Kaboodle online planner or consult with a Bunnings staff member for accurate pricing.

Are Kaboodle kitchens easy to install?

Kaboodle kitchens are designed to be DIY-friendly, with clear instructions and pre-drilled holes for easy assembly. However, the level of difficulty may vary depending on the individual's skill level and experience with DIY projects.

Are Kaboodle kitchens durable?

Kaboodle kitchens are made from quality materials and are designed to withstand normal wear and tear. However, the durability may depend on how well the kitchen is maintained and the usage it experiences.

Can I customize a Kaboodle kitchen to fit my space?

Yes, Kaboodle kitchens offer a range of customizable options, including different cabinet sizes, finishes, and accessories. This allows homeowners to create a kitchen that suits their specific needs and space requirements.

Can I add or change components of a Kaboodle kitchen after installation?

Yes, Kaboodle kitchens are designed to be flexible. You can easily add or change components such as cabinets, drawers, and benchtops even after installation. However, it's important to ensure compatibility with existing components.

Are Kaboodle kitchens good value for money?

The value of a Kaboodle kitchen depends on individual preferences and budget. It offers a cost-effective DIY solution for those who are willing to put in the effort of installation. However, it's recommended to compare prices and quality with other kitchen options before making a decision.

What is the warranty on Kaboodle kitchens?

Kaboodle kitchens come with a 10-year warranty on cabinets, hinges, drawers, and benchtops. This warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damage caused by improper installation or misuse.

How do customers feel about their experience with Kaboodle kitchens?

Customer experiences with Kaboodle kitchens can vary. It's advisable to read reviews and gather insights from other customers to get a better understanding of their satisfaction levels and any potential issues they encountered.

Can I hire professionals to install a Kaboodle kitchen?

Yes, Bunnings offers installation services through their network of professional installers. This can be a convenient option for those who prefer to have their Kaboodle kitchen installed by experts.
